yt-dlp -f "bv*[ext=mp4]+ba[ext=m4a]/b[ext=mp4]" URL

🧠 Why this works
bv*[ext=mp4]

    bv* = best video-only stream

    [ext=mp4] = must be in an mp4 container

    yt‑dlp automatically picks the highest resolution + bitrate

ba[ext=m4a]

    ba = best audio-only stream

    [ext=m4a] = must be m4a (the audio format used inside mp4)

/b[ext=mp4]

    fallback: if the site doesn’t offer separate video/audio streams, use the best single MP4 file
